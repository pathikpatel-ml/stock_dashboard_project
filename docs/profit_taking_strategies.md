### Winner vs Loser Management
- **Hold Winners Longer**: Research shows successful traders let profits run
- **Cut Losers Quickly**: 20% stop-loss preserves capital for better opportunities
- **Partial Profit-Taking**: Sell 25-50% at 20% gains, hold remainder for bigger moves

### Technical Analysis Integration
- **RSI Overbought (>70)**: Consider taking profits regardless of % gains
- **MACD Bearish Divergence**: Exit even profitable positions
- **Strong Volume**: Hold positions with increasing volume support

### Risk Management Rules
1. **Never Risk More Than 2%** of portfolio on single position
2. **20% Stop Loss** is maximum acceptable loss per trade
3. **Trailing Stops**: Move stop-loss up as profits increase
4. **Position Sizing**: Reduce size as portfolio grows

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
- **Loss Aversion**: Holding losers too long hoping to break even
- **Premature Profit-Taking**: Selling winners too early due to fear
- **Anchoring Bias**: Fixating on purchase price instead of current value

### Psychological Strategies
- **Pre-planned Exits**: Decide sell levels before buying
- **Systematic Approach**: Follow rules regardless of emotions
- **Portfolio Perspective**: Focus on overall performance, not individual trades
